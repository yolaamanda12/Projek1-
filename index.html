    About Us

    Yomary Community Health Center is a community health service center committed to providing high quality health care to our entire community. Since our founding, we have prioritized people's welfare and health needs as the main focus of our services. Therefore, we provide a wide range of primary health and disease prevention services designed to meet the health needs of individuals and families.

    Vision and mission

    Yomary Puskesmas has a vision, namely :

    • "To become a leading and trusted center for public health services, to be the main partner in improving the health and welfare of the community."

      • Yomary Health Center has a mission, namely:

    • Providing high quality and affordable health services to all levels of society.
    • Increase awareness of the importance of health and a healthy lifestyle through education and health promotion programs.
    • Create a supportive environment for our patients and staff, by providing friendly, skilled and empathetic service.
    • Collaborating closely with local governments, non-governmental organizations and other related parties to strengthen the public health system.

    With this vision and mission, we are committed to being a strong pillar in advancing the health and welfare of communities in our region, while continuing to adapt and develop to answer ever-growing health challenges.

    Yomary Puskesmas has a Community Health Program, namely:

    Health Education

    This program involves providing information and education to the public on various health topics, such as healthy eating patterns, personal hygiene, stress management, and treatment of certain diseases.

    Immunization

    The immunization program aims to increase immunization coverage in the community, including immunization of infants, toddlers, adolescents and adults, according to the recommended immunization schedule.

    Infectious Disease Prevention

    This program includes efforts to prevent the transmission of infectious diseases, such as HIV/AIDS, tuberculosis, and sexually transmitted infections (STIs), through education, testing, and promotion of healthy behavior.

    Reproductive Health and Family Planning Program

    This program provides reproductive health-related information and services, including counseling on contraceptive methods, pregnancy testing, and pre- and postnatal care.

    Services

    With the various services we provide, we aim to provide comprehensive, affordable and high-quality health care to our community. Here are some of our services:

    Management of Infectious and Non-Infectious Diseases

    Our medical team provides comprehensive treatment for various infectious and non-communicable diseases, including infectious diseases, hypertension, diabetes and other chronic diseases.

    General Health Checkup:

    We provide general health examination services to monitor health conditions regularly, carry out early diagnosis, and provide recommendations for further treatment if necessary.

    Maternal and Child Health Services:

    We provide special health services for pregnant women, babies and children, including pregnancy checks, nutritional consultations, immunizations and child health services

    Doctor Consultation

    Our team of doctors is ready to provide medical consultations for various health complaints, provide diagnoses, and provide appropriate advice and treatment.

    Laboratory Examination

    We provide laboratory examination services to help diagnose diseases and monitor health conditions, including blood tests, urine tests, and pathogen tests.

    Immunization Program

    We provide a complete immunization program for children and adults, according to the immunization schedule recommended by local health authorities.

    Frequently Asked Questioins

    "From several frequently asked questions to comprehensive explanations, this is a complete guide to understanding everything about the Yomary Health Center. Find answers to the public's frequently asked questions about the Yomary Health Center below."

    • What is meant by Yomary Health Center?

      Yomary Community Health Center is a community health center located in Depok, which provides primary health services to the surrounding community.

    • What services are available at Yomary Health Center?

      Yomary Health Center provides a variety of primary health services, including general health examinations, general medicine, cardiology, maternal and child health services, immunizations, and nutrition services.

    • How do I make an appointment with a doctor at Yomary Health Center?

      To make an appointment with a doctor at the Yomary Health Center, you can call the telephone number provided or come directly to the Puskesmas to register.

    • Does Yomary Health Center accept emergency patients?

      Yes, Yomary Health Center usually accepts emergency patients on a small scale and provides first aid before the patient is referred to a larger hospital if necessary.

    • How do I provide feedback or complaints about services at Yomary Community Health Center?

      You can provide feedback or complaints about services at the Yomary Health Center through the guest book, feedback form, or through communication facilities provided by the Yomary Health Center such as telephone or email.

    • What should I do if I am not satisfied with the service I received at Yomary Community Health Center?

      If you are not satisfied with the services you receive at the Yomary Community Health Center, you can submit an official complaint to the Yomary Community Health Center management or contact the relevant agency such as the local Health Service.
